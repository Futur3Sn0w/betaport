Announcement - August 13, 2022

“With wallpapers leveraged from Unsplash, you'll never be left with a stale wallpaper. If you want a switch up, you can click the Backdrop card to get a new image! Finally, you can filter wallpapers with a keyword in the Control Panel.”

“The shelf is home to all your cards. Cards have a 3D effect when hovered on desktop, reminiscent of the Apple TV home screen. Your cards showcase any information or shortcuts you select. Entering edit mode will allow you to enable or disable cards with a click/tap!”

“On touch-screen devices, the control panel is a swipe away. Just swipe in from the side to open or close it.”

“Another touchscreen feature is controlling the shelf with gestures. Swipe right/left on the backdrop card to move the shelf without going to the control panel. Additionally, you can touch+hold a card to enter edit mode.”
